Cappuccino – Step by Step

  1. Wake up the coffee beans and grind them like they owe you rent
  2. Pull a strong espresso shot — confidence is key
  3. Heat the milk gently (no boiling drama, please)
  4. Froth the milk until it looks cloud-soft
  5. Pour the espresso into the cup with style
  6. Add steamed milk slowly like a coffee artist
  7. Top with foam — the higher, the better
  8. Optional magic dust: cocoa or cinnamon

Sip. Smile. Pretend you’re in an Italian cafe
